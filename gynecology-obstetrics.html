About Our Department

At Prakruthi Hospital, we provide comprehensive, compassionate, and specialized care for women at every stage of life. Our department offers advanced diagnostic solutions, fertility support, pregnancy care, and cutting-edge laparoscopic procedures. With a patient-centered approach, we ensure safety, comfort, and ethical medical practices for every woman who trusts us with her health. Our department is equipped with modern ultrasound scanning, state-of-the-art operating theatres, and 24/7 emergency support, ensuring that patients receive timely and accurate medical care at any hour. We take pride in maintaining an environment that is safe, ethical, and patient-centered, where every woman feels heard, supported, and cared for. From routine consultations to complex surgical procedures, our goal is to deliver world-class women’s healthcare with utmost dedication and compassion.
