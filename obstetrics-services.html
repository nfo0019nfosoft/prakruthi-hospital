Obstetrics Services

Pregnancy Care • Safe Delivery • Mother & Baby Wellness

About Obstetrics Department

We provide complete pregnancy care from routine checkups to high-risk pregnancy management, fetal monitoring, painless delivery, cesarean section, and postnatal care with expert obstetricians.

Our Obstetrics Sub-Services

Routine Pregnancy Check-ups

Regular prenatal check-ups to ensure a healthy and safe pregnancy journey. During each visit, we monitor the baby’s growth, the mother’s health, and important pregnancy parameters. Our team provides dietary guidance, supplements, and preventive care to avoid complications. We ensure mothers feel supported, informed, and confident throughout every trimester.

BP & Sugar Monitoring
Monthly Consultations
Baby Movement Check

Fetal Ultrasound Scans

Accurate ultrasound scans to monitor fetal development and ensure safe pregnancy progress. These scans help confirm fetal growth, heart activity, placenta position, and amniotic fluid levels. We use high-quality imaging for detailed and reliable results. Timely scans allow early detection of concerns and help plan safe pregnancy care.

Early Pregnancy Scan
Anomaly Scan
Growth & Wellbeing Scan

High-Risk Pregnancy Care

Specialized monitoring and advanced management for high-risk pregnancies. Conditions such as hypertension, diabetes, thyroid disorders, or previous complications require close observation. We provide frequent check-ups, advanced testing, and emergency readiness to ensure safety. Our goal is to support mothers and babies with utmost care through a delicate pregnancy.

Gestational Diabetes Care
High BP / Thyroid Management
Intensive Antenatal Monitoring

Antenatal (Prenatal) Care

Complete antenatal care to protect the health of both mother and baby. This includes blood tests, supplements, vaccinations, and continuous health tracking. We guide mothers through nutrition, exercise, and emotional wellbeing during pregnancy. Early and consistent antenatal care helps prevent complications and ensures smooth delivery

Blood Tests & Scans
Diet & Lifestyle Guidance
Baby Growth Tracking

Ectopic Pregnancy

Immediate diagnosis and safe treatment for ectopic pregnancy to prevent complications. Ectopic pregnancy occurs when the embryo grows outside the uterus, requiring prompt medical care. We use ultrasound and clinical evaluation to confirm the condition quickly. Treatment is provided with utmost safety to preserve the future reproductive health of the mother.

Emergency Management
Medication or Surgery
Follow-up & Recovery Support

Normal Delivery

Safe and natural delivery with continuous monitoring and supportive care. Our team encourages natural birth whenever medically possible. Mothers receive guidance on breathing, pain management, and comfortable birthing positions. We ensure a calm, hygienic, and supportive environment for a positive birthing experience.

Mother-Focused Approach
Continuous Labor Support
Safe Birthing Environment

Painless / Epidural Delivery

Advanced epidural delivery for a comfortable and less painful childbirth experience. An experienced anesthesiologist administers the epidural for effective pain relief. Mothers remain awake, comfortable, and fully involved during birth. This technique ensures safety, reduced stress, and a smooth delivery process.

Epidural Pain Relief
Comfortable Labor
Safe for Mother & Baby

Cesarean Section (C-Section)

Safe and expert surgical delivery when normal birth is not advisable. Performed by skilled surgeons using modern techniques to ensure minimal pain and quick recovery. We prioritize the health of both mother and baby during every step. Post-surgery follow-up care is provided to support healing and comfort.

Planned & Emergency C-Section
Modern Operation Theatres
Fast Post-Delivery Recovery

Fetal Growth & Health Monitoring

Regular monitoring of fetal growth, movements, and overall wellbeing. We use ultrasound, Doppler studies, and clinical evaluations to assess fetal health accurately. This helps detect growth restrictions or complications early. Our monitoring ensures timely intervention for the safest outcomes.

Doppler Scans
Growth Tracking
Placenta & Fluid Analysis

Post-delivery Recovery Support

Complete postnatal care to help mothers regain strength, heal faster, and stay emotionally healthy. Support includes wound care, feeding guidance, and hormonal balance checks. We educate mothers about newborn care and self-care routines. Our team ensures a smooth transition into motherhood with continuous support.

Newborn Feeding Guidance
Postpartum Check-ups
Emotional & Physical Recovery
