About Our Surgical Department

Prakruthi Hospital provides state-of-the-art surgical care with a strong focus on minimally invasive laparoscopic procedures. Our department is equipped with advanced operation theatres, high-end imaging technology, and experienced surgeons who specialize in performing safe, precise, and effective surgeries. We ensure faster recovery, reduced pain, and minimal scars for our patients.
